This contributor agreement ("Agreement") documents the rights granted by the corporation and its employees to Us. This is a legally binding document, so please have the authorized representative of your corporation carefully review and execute this Agreement before submitting contributions. The Agreement may cover more than one software project managed by Us.

- "Contribution" means any work of authorship, including any associated documentation, bug reports, and feature requests that is submitted by the Corporation or on the Corporation's behalf to Us in connection with the Work.
- "Corporation" means the legal entity executing this Agreement and includes all other entities that control, are controlled by, or are under common control with that entity.
- "Employees" means any individuals who are employed by the Corporation or any of its subsidiaries.
- "Individual Agreement" means an Individual Contributor License Agreement executed by a representative of the Employee.
- "Us" (or "We") means FlashQuery Inc. and its subsidiaries and affiliates.
- "Work" means the FlashQuery software and related documentation.
