| T-146-17 | Denial of Service | pg pool exhaustion | mitigate | Use process-scoped pool, release in `finally`, and test 20 concurrent writes. |
| T-146-18 | Information Disclosure | SQL/log errors | mitigate | Log connection/release failures without raw record field content or credentials. |

</threat_model>
