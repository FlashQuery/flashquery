## Resolved Critical Gap

### INT-001: Mixed-scope background lifecycle job lifetime

Affected requirements: REQ-035, REQ-036, REQ-038, REQ-039, REQ-041.

For `maintain_vault({ action: "backfill_embeddings" | "rebuild_embeddings", background: true, scope: { entity_types: ["documents", "memory", "records"] } })`, dispatch prepares and returns one public lifecycle job. Mixed processors now keep that job running across both core and records phases, then complete it once with combined counts.

Evidence:

| File | Evidence |
|---|---|
| `src/embedding/lifecycle/core-processor.ts` | `runCoreLifecycle` accepts `finalizeJob:false` for caller-owned public jobs and heartbeats instead of completing during the core phase. |
| `src/embedding/lifecycle/backfill.ts` | mixed background backfill defers completion to the wrapper, reuses the public job for records, and combines counts. |
| `src/embedding/lifecycle/rebuild.ts` | mixed background rebuild defers completion to the wrapper, reuses the public job for records, and combines counts. |
| `tests/unit/lifecycle-mixed-background-job.test.ts` | proves mixed backfill and rebuild do not complete after core alone and complete once after records with combined counts. |
